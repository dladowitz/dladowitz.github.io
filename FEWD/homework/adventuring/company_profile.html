Mountain High Adventures

Company Info

Mountian High Adventures offers an array of rock climbing instruction, rock climbing lessons and private guiding in the form of one day rock climbing classes and two to four day rock climbing courses and rock climbing seminars ensuring a perfect fit for you. From the beginner through advanced rock climber or indoor rock climbing gym enthusiast looking to further their skills to achieve higher goals. Thoughts of climbing multi pitch routes at Tahquitz Rock, Yosemite Valley California, Red Rock Nevada or developing big wall climbing techniques for climbing Yosemite's El Capitan or an excursion on a long moderate high country classic Sierra rock climb maybe where your spirit guides you. Either way, world class climbing instruction in sunny California with one of our personable certified professional rock climbing guides is your avenue to a safe, fun and educational rock climbing future.
